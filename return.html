Return & Refund Policy

Shop worry-free at EchoShop! We’ve made returns and refunds simple and hassle-free.

Last updated: July 31, 2025

1. Eligibility for Returns

Products are eligible for return within 7 to 10 days from delivery. Items must be:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all tags intact
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order ID or invoice)

2. How to Return an Item

  1. Go to "My Orders" in your EchoShop account
  2. Click on the item you want to return
  3. Select a return reason and upload a photo (optional)
  4. Confirm pickup address
  5. Hand over the product to our delivery partner

You'll receive SMS/email updates during each step.

3. Non-returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned unless received damaged:

  • Innerwear, personal care & hygiene products
  • Gift cards
  • Custom-made products
  • Digital goods

4. Refund Timeline

All refunds are made to the original payment method used.

Payment Mode Refund Time
UPI 1–3 business days
Card/Netbanking 5–7 business days
Cash on Delivery UPI refund link or bank transfer (5–7 days)

5. Exchange Policy

  • Exchanges are allowed only if the item is defective or wrongly delivered.
  • Exchange request can be raised in the My Orders section.
  • Exchange is subject to stock availability.
